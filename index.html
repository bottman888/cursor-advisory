03

How we work.

Three engagements, in order. Start with the assessment. The rest is optional, on your terms.

01

AI Opportunity Assessment

FIXED FEE · REMOTE

Three weeks. We interview your team, map your current operations, and deliver a prioritised roadmap with ROI estimates for each opportunity. You leave knowing exactly where to start, and the roadmap is written to be actionable whether you implement it with us or not.

At its centre is the Ops Baseline, a visual map of how your business actually runs, including where things slow down, repeat, or fall through the cracks. That clarity is valuable before a single thing is automated.

02

Implementation

FIXED FEE · PER PROJECT

For clients who want to act on the roadmap, we scope and deliver specific projects on a fixed-cost basis from targeted quick wins through to larger workflow builds. We manage technical execution, team training, and the change itself. You stay focused on the business.

03

Ongoing Advisory

Monthly retainer

A monthly retainer that keeps us in your corner. Your AI person — someone who understands your business, available without the overhead of a full-time hire. Strategic input, accountability for what's been built, and support as things evolve.
